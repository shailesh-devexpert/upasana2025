COURSE LOCATION

Join Us for a 100-Hour Traditional Yoga Teacher Training at Begnas Lake Resort, Pokhara, Nepal

Set against the stunning backdrop of the Himalayas and nestled beside a serene forest lake, our 100-hour Traditional Yoga Teacher Training is a truly transformative experience. Held at the Begnas Lake Resort, this majestic haven was created out of a deep love for pure nature, natural remedies, wholesome food, and the therapeutic power of these elements combined. Here, you’ll immerse yourself in the tranquil beauty of the Nepali countryside, free from the hustle and bustle of daily life, while cultivating a deeper connection to your practice and self.

Our resort is a hidden paradise, buffered by a lush tropical jungle on three sides and the serene Begnas Lake on the fourth, making it a sanctuary of peace and solitude. Despite its remote feel, it’s conveniently located just 11 km from Pokhara City, making it easily accessible yet perfectly secluded. You can reach the resort by a 10-minute forest walk from Sundari Danda or, for a more serene arrival, take a relaxing boat ride from Begnas Bazar boat station.

During your training, you’ll be welcomed by our warm, local staff who are always ready to ensure your comfort and well-being. Our main restaurant offers a large, airy dining area with spectacular views of the surrounding mountains, as well as a cozy bar. For larger groups, we have two conference halls that can accommodate up to 70 people and provide facilities for meals and events for over 100 guests.

In this tranquil setting, you’ll experience the profound peace and beauty of nature, with opportunities to relax by our chemical-free pool, meditate by the pristine lake, or explore the surrounding forest and villages. Our location is perfect for hiking, village walks, mountain biking, paragliding, and trail running, allowing you to connect with nature in every way. And if you ever miss urban life, Pokhara City is just a short drive away.

This yoga teacher training is not just about learning the traditional teachings of yoga—it’s about living them in an environment that supports holistic well-being. It’s an opportunity to reconnect with nature, deepen your practice, and transform your life.

We look forward to welcoming you to this beautiful corner of Nepal, where the wisdom of yoga and the peace of the mountains await you.

For the yoga teacher training, please bring comfortable clothing suitable for yoga practice, as well as warm clothes for the cooler temperatures. What kind of yoga styles are taught at Karmayogi’s yoga teacher Training 100 hrs in Pokhara Nepal?

Our yoga teacher training offers heritage of traditional Hatha Yoga, Ashtanga yoga. The focus is on internal awareness, good alignment practices, and a comprehensive understanding of yoga. You’ll also learn to create your sequences, practice pre-designed sequences, and delve into subtle energy practices, pranayama, and meditation.

How much emphasis is placed on teaching methodology and practice during the YTT 100 program?

Our courses are highly interactive and practical. We provide numerous opportunities for teaching practice, including two practical exams with feedback from both peers and experienced tutors. This ensures your development as a confident and capable yoga teacher.

FAQs - Yoga School in Rishikesh India

What are the languages preferred during the course for communication & teaching at Upasana Yoga School in Rishikesh?

We prefer to communicate in English since we receive students from across the globe, so it becomes easier to converse in English. But we also love to teach some important words from the Hindi language since it is widely spoken in Rishikesh, like Namaskara (Hello), Dhanyawaad (Thank you), Swagatam (Welcome), and many more. 

Do you charge extra for learning material, or is the cost inclusive in the fee?

We provide books, manuals, and other essential study materials, including the cost you pay during the course. We don't charge anything extra. 

How can I reach the premises of Upasana Yoga School in Rishikesh?

It depends on the mode of transportation and the place you are coming from. Although we have provided the complete address on the website, you can reach us here by bus, train, cab, and through flight to Dehradun Airport. Rishikesh is 1.30 hours away from Dehradun by taxi or bus. 

Is there medical support or assistance available at school?

Yes, we take every necessary measure for our students regarding their health. We have connections with local doctors who are available for immediate assistance, along with a medicare team available 24*7 for our students and teachers. 

Are we allowed gadgets during the class?

You can carry all types of gadgets except those that are harmful or a threat to anyone. Mobile phones, laptops, and tabs can be used only during the classes once and if asked by the school's teachers. 

How many students are there in a single batch of a particular course?

There are usually 14-17 students in a single batch of a particular course. 

Which Visa should I apply for, and does Upasana Yoga School help us avail?

You can apply for a tourist visa. As your chosen school for YTTC, it is our responsibility to guide and help you with visa assistance, but we aren't fully responsible for the same. We are limited to verbal assistance only. But if you apply for an e-tourist visa, that's even better and easy. Apply for a minimum of 30 days of tourist visa if you are applying for individual courses like 200 hour YTTC or 300 hour YTTC, but if you are applying for 500 hour YTTC, you should apply for a minimum of 60 days. 

Do I need to bring my own yoga equipment for the course?

No, we provide a complete yoga kit; all you have to bring along is the passion for learning & dedication toward the course.

What type of courses does Upasana Yoga School provide to students?

200 Hour Yoga Teacher Training Course, 200 Hour Yoga Teacher Training Course, 300 Hour Yoga TTC, 500 Hour Yoga TTC, Yin-Yang Yoga, Prenatal and Postnatal YTTC, Aerial Yoga, and other Yoga Retreats. 

Do you provide complete course details for different training courses?

Each training program is designed according to the structure and respected process. We constantly inform students about their classes, timings, facilities available, and everything covered under the course during that time. It is our duty to maintain transparency and let our students feel more confident in their learning journey.
