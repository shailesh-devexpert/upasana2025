DAY-TO-DAY EVENTS IN 7 Days Basic Ayurveda & Yoga Retreat in Rishikesh

Day 1-

  • Welcome
  • Pooja and fire ceremony.
  • Course Overview and Distribution of Material.
  • Basic Principles and Introduction to Ayurveda, Doshas, Body Types and Panchakarma.
  • Followed by our Freshly prepared warm herbal tea/ Hibiscus tea.

Day 2-

  • Jalneti – One of the Shatkarmas in Yogic Cleansing. The second day will begin with the practice of Jal-neti early in the morning, one of the Shatkarmas of Yoga. This cleanses your channels and provides clarity of senses, thereby preparing your mind and body for the upcoming practice of Pranayama and Ayurveda every day.
  • Pranayam and Yoga class: A Pranayama and Yoga session follows this. We introduce a new theme every day, throughout our retreat to ensure we are working on physical, mental and spiritual aspects of your well-being.
  • Marma therapy: A class on Marma therapy, followed by a Marma massage where you get to experience its wonders.

Day 3-

  • Detox yoga flow
  • Introduction to Rasayana(Rejuvenation)Therapy, types of Rasayana, it’s preparation and tasting of various Rasayanas and brain tonics available in Ayurveda.
  • Therapy: Kayaseka/ Abhyanga + Swedana /Udhwarthana customized according to body type

Day 4-

  • After having worked on your Chakras, Day 4 will be all about working on your senses. Our Pancha jyanendriya (sense organs) are the receptors of continuous stimulus in our body. It is therefore essential to soothe our senses and revitalize them with our chosen Ayurvedic practices.
  • Tarpana therapy
  • Eye exercises
  • Trataka Meditation
  • Introduction to Chakras and Mudras followed by Performing of Chandra Namaskar

Day 5-

  • A day trip to the beautiful Mountains Of Rishikesh

Day 6-

  • Relaxing Yoga Flow
  • Lecture on the Importance of Agni/Digestive Fire and Eating Habits of a Yogi.
  • Shirodhara

Day 7-

  • Morning Pranayam and Yoga session
  • Pranayam and Yoga class: A Pranayama and Yoga session follows this. We introduce a new theme every day, throughout our retreat to ensure we are working on physical, mental and spiritual aspects of your well-being.
  • Farewell and checkout
