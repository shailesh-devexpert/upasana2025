FAQs - 100 Hour Yoga Teacher Training in Rishikesh

Are there any prerequisites to join the course?

There are no conditions except you have to be on time to avoid any hassle or later complications. As long as you are dedicated to your course & have the zeal to establish yourself as a successful yoga trainer, you remain unstoppable and undefeated. Learning 100 hour of YTTC will surely be a transformative journey. 

Will I be certified to become a yoga trainer after completing this course?

Yes, once you have completed this 100-hour YTTC and been given oral and written examinations (passed them), you are eligible to receive a certificate from us that the Yoga Alliance, USA, also recognizes. 

Can I start teaching immediately after this course? 

No matter where you are, you can begin teaching as a yoga teacher trainer after completing your course. 

Can beginners be a part of this 100 hrs YTTC in Rishikesh?

Yes, beginners are most welcome. 

Is it necessary to pursue teaching after the course is completed?

No, there is no compulsion to become a yoga teacher and train students. It depends on your interest and willingness. Most students want to become a teacher, whereas many are here to experience yoga in general or for knowledge. 

Do I need to bring anything like a mat or yoga equipment? 

No, you don't have to carry anything with you, but if you are extremely specific about your choices or have personal reasons, you can discuss this with our team before taking a final call. 

What can I learn under this 100-hour YTTC by Upasana Yoga School in Rishikesh?

You will learn anatomy, philosophy, mantra chanting, Hatha Yoga, Pranayama, Ashtanga Yoga, Power Yoga and much more. Check our 100 hrs YTTC page for more information.

What if I have some emergency healthcare problem during the course?

Although we have a medicare team with us in school who regularly checks our student's health in case of emergency, we are always connected to our local doctors for any problem. Also, we have all the medicines to offer except those not prescribed to keep without special permission. We advise you to keep us informed about any chronic health problem so that we can take precautions in our service. 

Is vegan food available?

Yes, we provide vegan food to our students, and in case of allergies, we take extra measures to be more dietary-specific in serving individuals. You can convey us in advance. 

Is this 100-hour YTTC by Upasana Yoga School in Rishikesh difficult?

Nothing is difficult if you truly want to pursue it. With experienced world-class teachers, you will be taught to handle pressure and strike a balance of advanced learning with much ease and comfort. So don't worry, stay excited. 

Is Rishikesh Safe for Travellers?

Yes, Rishikesh is a safe place, especially for tourists. It is a safer destination since it is a preferable spot for yoga courses. It is tourist-friendly, and there is cultural sensitivity that makes it even better for everyone who is traveling from abroad. The atmosphere is spiritual and tranquil & the most important thing is that everyone is helpful and will always greet you with a warm welcome. But it is always advisable to stay informed and attentive to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. 

What is the quality of food and drinking water?

The food quality is excellent at our school premises, and you get to drink filtered or distilled water. We take premium care when it comes to the health and hygiene of our students. There is absolutely no compromise. 

How do you buy a SIM card there?

We advise you to get the sim card from our school since that is preferable according to the location you are staying at. So basically, there are no network problems at all if you buy the sim from our reception. You simply have to connect with our school manager, who is available at the school's reception. You buy the SIM card immediately from there, and it shall be activated within a few minutes. So, don't be worried, we will take care of it. But just in case you prefer buying from somewhere else, it is your choice. 

Do we need any vaccination?

No, you need no vaccination to learn yoga from our school. 

Is language a barrier during a medical emergency?

Most people know how to converse in English in Rishikesh, especially in hospitals and pharmacies, but if you speak a different language, it might be challenging to communicate. But don't worry if there is a medical emergency; our staff will always be there with you. We will support you throughout the process so you don't get stuck with other things. 

Is food spicy? Is vegan food available?

Food at Upasana Yoga School is vegetarian, and the spice level is either medium or low. We don't want to hurt anyone's tummy, so we keep it tasty, healthy, and less spicy. One that suits everyone. Vegan food is available, and if you have specific demands, let us know in advance. 

Is there a free day between the course?

Sundays are usually free, so you get to rest, roam around, and enjoy as per your wish. 

Will I gain a spiritual awakening during the course?

Definitely, you are in the city of Sages, and our school understands the importance of spiritual presence in one's life. One of the most important aspects of our courses is the existence of spirituality. Some topics in our courses are genuinely dedicated to spirituality, which greatly influences an individual's progress. 

Is travel insurance necessary?

No, travel insurance is not required. 

If stomach sickness occurs, then are the medical fees too expensive?

We hope nothing like this happens to you, but medical expenses are not overwhelming just in case anything like this happens. Also, India is known for its brilliant healthcare system, and in Rishikesh, we have the best medical hospital, AIIMS, which is a world renowned hospital with the best doctors. 

Can I pay fees through a credit card?

Yes, you can pay your fees through a credit card. We accept International Credit and Debit cards for transactions. 

Any age limit for this course?

Yes, ages 10 to 65 are mostly advisable, even as beginners, but we always recommend that our students take care of their health before joining any yoga course. But we also know that age is nothing but a mental restriction, so as long as you are ready to take the challenge, and experience hard-core body movements and some tricky moves, don't think twice & join in. 

Are there any restrictions on clothes or attire to be worn during the course?

You can keep it aesthetic; you can keep it classy! We recommend our students wear traditional or comfortable clothes on the school premises, but we have a slight restriction on wearing short or seamless attire because it doesn't suit the tradition at the school. Outside school, feel free to wear anything.

Can I still enroll if I have some ongoing injury or have a specific health problem? 

Feel free to join the course if the injury is not severe and prolonged. But we advise you to only take the course if you feel completely healthy.

FAQs - Yoga School in Rishikesh India

What are the languages preferred during the course for communication & teaching at Upasana Yoga School in Rishikesh?

We prefer to communicate in English since we receive students from across the globe, so it becomes easier to converse in English. But we also love to teach some important words from the Hindi language since it is widely spoken in Rishikesh, like Namaskara (Hello), Dhanyawaad (Thank you), Swagatam (Welcome), and many more. 

Do you charge extra for learning material, or is the cost inclusive in the fee?

We provide books, manuals, and other essential study materials, including the cost you pay during the course. We don't charge anything extra. 

How can I reach the premises of Upasana Yoga School in Rishikesh?

It depends on the mode of transportation and the place you are coming from. Although we have provided the complete address on the website, you can reach us here by bus, train, cab, and through flight to Dehradun Airport. Rishikesh is 1.30 hours away from Dehradun by taxi or bus. 

Is there medical support or assistance available at school?

Yes, we take every necessary measure for our students regarding their health. We have connections with local doctors who are available for immediate assistance, along with a medicare team available 24*7 for our students and teachers. 

Are we allowed gadgets during the class?

You can carry all types of gadgets except those that are harmful or a threat to anyone. Mobile phones, laptops, and tabs can be used only during the classes once and if asked by the school's teachers. 

How many students are there in a single batch of a particular course?

There are usually 14-17 students in a single batch of a particular course. 

Which Visa should I apply for, and does Upasana Yoga School help us avail?

You can apply for a tourist visa. As your chosen school for YTTC, it is our responsibility to guide and help you with visa assistance, but we aren't fully responsible for the same. We are limited to verbal assistance only. But if you apply for an e-tourist visa, that's even better and easy. Apply for a minimum of 30 days of tourist visa if you are applying for individual courses like 100 hour YTTC or 300 hour YTTC, but if you are applying for 500 hour YTTC, you should apply for a minimum of 60 days. 

Do I need to bring my own yoga equipment for the course?

No, we provide a complete yoga kit; all you have to bring along is the passion for learning & dedication toward the course.

What type of courses does Upasana Yoga School provide to students?

100 Hour Yoga Teacher Training Course, 100 Hour Yoga Teacher Training Course, 300 Hour Yoga TTC, 500 Hour Yoga TTC, Yin-Yang Yoga, Prenatal and Postnatal YTTC, Aerial Yoga, and other Yoga Retreats. 

Do you provide complete course details for different training courses?

Each training program is designed according to the structure and respected process. We constantly inform students about their classes, timings, facilities available, and everything covered under the course during that time. It is our duty to maintain transparency and let our students feel more confident in their learning journey.
